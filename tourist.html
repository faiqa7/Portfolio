Favourite Tourist Spots

"Dubai, UAE"

Dubai is a city and emirate in the United Arab Emirates known for luxury shopping, ultramodern architecture and a lively nightlife scene. Burj Khalifa dominates the skyscraper-filled skyline. It is one of the world's leading tourism destinations and tourism in Dubai is a major source of revenue.

"Cappadocia, Turkey"

Cappadocia is a semi-arid region in Turkey, it is known for its distinctive “fairy chimneys,” tall, cone-shaped rock formations clustered in Monks Valley, Göreme and elsewhere. Other notables sites include Bronze Age homes carved into valley walls and later used as refuges. It attracts large number of tourists.

"Singapore"

Singapore is the Republic of Singapore, is a sovereign island country. It is famous for being a global financial center, being among the most densely populated places, having a world-class city airport with a waterfall, and a Botanic Garden. It is known as a tourist destination and for its hot and humid climate.

"Paris, France"

Paris is the capital and most populous city of France, the 34th most densely populated city in the world in 2020. Since the 17th century, Paris has been one of the world's major centres of finance, diplomacy, commerce, fashion, gastronomy, science, and arts. Tourism is the major source of income of France.
